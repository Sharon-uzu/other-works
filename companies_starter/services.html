Our Company Services

We provide high quality technical services to our customers and business partners in the Marine and Land sector.

Repair and maintenance

Owners placing their repair or service orders with ALLIMACOTECH, can be rest assured that their vessels are in the best hands. Our team of dedicated service coordinators, who all have a seagoing career behind them, knows what is best for your vessels, and what to do to get your vessels up and running in shortest possible time while keeping the cost at a minimum, keeping in mind your vessels limited service budgets.

Installations/Commissioning

Installation, commissioning and maintenance of marine radio communications systems and Global Maritime Distress &Safety System, emergency position indicating radio beacon, shore based GMDSS maintenance services, long range information and tracking systems, fire alarm system, Supply, samsung automation and control systems, satellite communications for land based and marine operations. We are highly skilled in the provision, installation, integration and commissioning of Gyro stabilized C-band and Ku-band antennas for VSAT networks

Procurement/supply

CCTV for ships with night vision facility. Gyro compass, Radars ARPA and Auto Pilot. Electronic Chart and Data Information System (ECDIS) and Echo Sounders. Voyage Data Recorder (VDR) and Automatic Information System (AIS) Integrated Bridge Solutions (IBS) and Ship Security Alarm System (SSAS) Electrical services/Rewinding of electric motors Satcom Equipment, Satcom Software and Radios Vessel Tracking System and Long Range Thermal Camera.
