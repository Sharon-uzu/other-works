Frequently Asked Questions

How can I invest with therealworldftrading.org Limited ?
First of all, please create an account with therealworldftrading.org Limited if you haven't yet created one. Then log into your account using your username and password and go to the deposit page where you should choose an investment package and make a deposit.

Yes. Our company is duly registered in Great Britain. Please click here to check the company on the registrar website by its registration number 10398666.
Is your website safe?
Yes. Our website is well protected against DDoS attacks, SSL encryption with extended validation which ensures the strongest encryption of data transferred from your browser to our server. Our team includes qualified system administrators to ensure safe and stable functioning of our website.
I wish to invest with therealworldftrading.org Limited, but I don't have an any e-currency account. How can I invest in my case?
You can open a free account with any of these e-currencies:
Perfect Money: www.perfectmoney.is
Payeer: www.payeer.com
Bitcoin: www.blockchain.info
AdvCash: www.advcash.com
How do I create my Account with therealworldftrading.org Limited?
Please click here, fill in the registration form and then press "Register".
Which e-currencies do you accept?
We accept BitCoin, PerfectMoney, Payeer and AdvCash.
How can I withdraw funds?
Log into your account using your username and password and then go to the withdrawal page.
How soon will my deposit be created after I make payment?
All deposits are created instantly. If you pay with Bitcoin, your deposit will be created after three confirmations.
What if I can't log into my account because I forgot my password?
Please go to the password recovery page, type in your username or e-mail follow further instructions.
Will I be receiving my profit to my e-currency account?
No, profit is accrued on your therealworldftrading.org Limited account balance and you can withdraw your available balance to your e-currency account.
May I invest directly from my account balance?
Yes. Log into your account, go to the deposit page, specify the investment plan and deposit amount and in the e-currency list select e-currency option of your account balance which has sufficient funds on it.
How soon will my withrawal requests be processed?
We do our best to process all withdrawal requests as soon as possible. But since all requests are processed manually, you will be receiving your requested funds within 1-36 business hours.
How can I change my password?
Please log into your account and then go to the preferences page.
How can I check my account balance?
You can access your account information any time, via the Internet.
May I open several accounts in your program?
No. If we find out that the same member has more that one account, all funds on the accounts in question will be frozen.
Who manages the funds?
We employ highly qualified financial experts with a positive track of records.
